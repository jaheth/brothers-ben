Brothers Ben is committed to providing open, honest and affordable web design for the small business owner

Brothers Ben aims to offer affordable, open, and honest web design services to their clients. We mainly focus on small business, but are open to any project. If you’ve had a wordpress site built for you five years ago that you just can’t seem to make any headway on, you’re looking for an affordable platform to market your business, or you need a space to turn your passion projects into revenue, we’re your guys. Whether it’s a brand new site, a new serviced solution, or an in-depth overhaul of your existing website, we’ve got you covered.

Meet the Brothers Ben. They met online in the late 2000’s, and became fast friends. After a trip to Japan together, there was no separating them. Both being keenly interested in web design and business, they started getting requests from their friends to help out with their own projects. They discovered quickly that a lot of small businesses are taken advantage of, paying huge prices for websites that depreciate quickly, become out of date and end up being more of a liability than an asset. At that point, Brothers Ben was born…
